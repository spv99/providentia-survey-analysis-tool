Univariate analysis involves the analysis of a single variable (or column) to describe, summarise and find patterns.

Bivariate analysis involves the study of two variables to determine the relationship between them and the strength of their correlation.

Multivariate analysis occurs when three or more variables are being analysed, having multiple intercorrelations and relationships between each variable.
